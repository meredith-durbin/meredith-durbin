Astrobiology in social contexts

This is a work-in-progress collection of readings broadly related to astrobiology in social, historical, and philosophical contexts. While many of them take explicitly critical and/or decolonial positions, some don't; I've included those because they may be illustrative of views and narratives common within the field.
