About Me

I'm currently a fourth year astrophysics major at Pomona College. My senior thesis is with Carnegie Observatories on the mid-infrared period-luminosity relation for RR Lyrae variable stars—read about it on Astrobites! I'm hoping to work for at least a few years before possibly looking into pursuing an astrophysics Ph.D.

I am co-founder of ALPhA ("Awesome Ladies in Physics and Astronomy") along with two other women in my physics class, and have previously mentored for the Claremont Colleges Queer/Questioning/Allied Mentor Program.

When not working on my thesis, I can be found writing for The Daily Geekette and playing around with Photoshop.
