Hi there! I'm a recent addition to the WFC3 team on HST, and have just started work on the IR detector and on QuickLook, the database of in-flight WFC3 images. (This page will be updated with more information about my recent work soon!)

I graduated from Pomona College in May 2014 with a BA in astrophysics. My senior thesis was on the RR Lyrae period-luminosity relation in IRAC channels 1 and 2, supervised by Dr. Victoria Scowcroft at Carnegie Observatories and Dr. Alma Zook at Pomona. This work was presented as a poster at AAS224, and we are currently preparing it for publication; in the meantime, my thesis is available here, or you can read the condensed version on Astrobites. I've also observed with the 1-meter telescope at JPL's Table Mountain Observatory to obtain optical blazar polarimetry data under the supervision of Dr. Alma Zook; the results of this research were presented at AAS221.

If you're interested in working with me, feel free to see what I can do and drop me a line.
