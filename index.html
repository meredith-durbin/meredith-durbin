About Me

I'm currently a fourth year at Pomona College studying astrophysics and looking for work in various industries. My current work is with Carnegie Observatories. on characterizing the mid-infrared period-luminosity relation for RR Lyrae stars. I'm currently hoping to work in industry for a few years before pursuing a PhD.

I'm particularly interested in science writing and the role of media in science communication, including how popular media influences perceptions of science and scientists along lines of gender and race.

I am co-founder of ALPhA ("Awesome Ladies in Physics and Astronomy") along with two other women in my physics class, and have previously mentored for the Claremont Colleges Queer/Questioning/Allied Mentor Program.
